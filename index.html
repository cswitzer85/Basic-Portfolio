About Me

My name is Cody Switzer, I'm married (Judy) and have an 18 month old son (Elliott). I was born in Denton, Texas and have lived in North Texas my entire life. I love to visit new places, nationally and internationally. Whenever I'm visiting somewhere new, I like to immerese myself into the culture and blend in (as best as I can) so that I can gain a better understanding of the people and what it is like to live there. I love to try new foods and recreate them back home. Food is a great way to find common ground with strangers and even if you don't have similar tastes, you can learn from them and speak your mind about what drives your preferences.
